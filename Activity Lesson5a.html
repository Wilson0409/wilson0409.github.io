Simple Sponge Cafe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Castor sugar

Finely granulated white sugar

Self raising flour

A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents casually salt and baking powder



Method

  1. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  2. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  3. Fold in flour.
  4. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven until the top of the cake spring back when lighty pressed
  6. Cool in the pan over a wire rack
