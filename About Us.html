Company Background


Founded in 2005, ComfyGuard has been a trusted company in the child safety industry. The company was established by a group of passionate entrepreneurs who recognized the need for innovative, reliable, and affordable car seats for parents. With a deep understanding of the importance of child safety, we set out to create a company that prioritizes the well-being of the young passengers on the road. Today, ComfyGuard has a strong presence in the global market, with distribution channels across authorized retailers, specialty baby stores, and online platforms. We continue to expland our product offerings by introducing new designs that align with constant evolving industry standards and consumer preferences.




Aunthentication & Warranty


The four products of ComfyGuard are certified by Malaysian Institute of Road Safety Research (MIROS) and comply to the United Nations Regulations (UNR) standards R44 and R129. All car seats will have a 3-years warranty, covering manufacturing defects and workmanship.
